Welcome to Acton Cleanup Week

For over twenty years, different hard-working citizens, town staff and business owners have been organizing, running, and supporting a spring-time clean-up in Acton. This year Green Acton and the Acton Chinese American Civic Society have organized something new: a week-long cleanup time for residents to help clean up trash in public places around the town. We invite everyone to participate.

Click here to register. Once registered, you can use the interactive map of Acton to identify areas that need cleaning or sign up for areas to clean. Check out this tutorial if you aren't sure how to use the map.

We encourage everyone to sign up for a site and clean it up — pick up any trash and/or recyclable items you may find. You can also report areas that require cleaning.

This service for your community can be done any time from April 22 to April 29 (Saturday to Saturday). On the last day, Saturday April 29, there will be volunteers from 10 AM to 2 PM to hand out empty bags, collect full bags, and help you locate places that need cleaning. They will be at two locations:

  • Acton Memorial Library, on the Main Street side
  • Jones Tavern in South Acton, courtesy of Iron Work Farm, Inc.

Bags for trash and recycling can be picked up from the Acton Memorial Library (486 Main Street). Students in your family can also pick up bags from Acton elementary schools or the junior and senior high schools.

Acton's recreation areas can be used during clean-up week to drop off trash bags with street trash:

  • Concord Rd. Field: (104 Concord Rd.)
  • Elm St. Fields (21 Elm Street)
  • Gardner Field (530 Massachusetts Avenue: in West Acton near Kinsley Rd)
  • Goward Playground (486 Main Street: behind Town Hall and the Momorial Library)
  • Great Hill (54 School Street: behind the fire station)
  • Hart Field / MacPherson Field (80 Taylor Road: at the Conant Elementary Schoo)
  • Jones Field (54 Martin Street)
  • MacPherson Field (80 Taylor Road: at the Conant Elementary School.)
  • Morrison Property (116 Concord Road: between the cemetery and Ice House Pond)
  • NARA Park (25 Ledge Rock Way: off Rte 27 in North Acton)
  • School St. Field (343-347 School Street)
  • T.J. O’Grady Skate Park (66 Hayward Road: near the ABRHS)
  • Veteran’s Field (655 Main Street: at the intersection of 2A and Route 27.

Thank you in advance for your support of and participation in Acton Cleanup Week to make Acton a more beautiful and enjoyable place in which we all can live and play!

Any questions? Contact the team at cleanup@greenacton.org
