Welcome to Acton Cleanup Week

Green Acton has organized a week-long cleanup activity for residents to help clean up trash in public places around the town. We invite everyone to participate.

Click here to register. Once registered, you can use the interactive map of Acton to sign up for areas to clean.

We encourage everyone to sign up for a site and clean it up â€” pick up any trash and/or recyclable items you may find. You can also report areas that require cleaning.

This service for your community can be done any time from April 22 to April 29 (Saturday to Saturday).

Bags for trash and recycling can be picked up from the Acton Memorial Library (486 Main Street). Students in your family can also pick up bags from Acton elementary schools or the junior and senior high schools.

Thank you in advance for your support of and participation in Acton Cleanup Week to make Acton a more beautiful and enjoyable place in which we all can live and play!
