Welcome to Acton Cleanup Week

For the past several years, Green Acton has organized a cleanup day for residents to help clean up trash in public places around the town. This year, Green Acton and Acton Chinese American Civic Society join forces to expand the one-day event into a town-wide, week-long activity in which we invite everyone to participate.

Click here to register. The map on this site displays specific areas in Acton that need attention.

We encourage everyone to sign up for a site and clean it up â€” pick up any trash and/or recyclable items you may find. You can also add other areas that need cleanup. This service for your community can be done any time from April 22 through April 29 (Saturday to Saturday). Bags for trash and recycling can be picked up from the Acton Memorial Library (486 Main Street). Students in your family can also pick up bags from Acton elementary schools or the junior and senior high schools.

Thank you in advance for your support of and participation in Acton Cleanup Week to make Acton a more beautiful and enjoyable place in which we all can live and play!
